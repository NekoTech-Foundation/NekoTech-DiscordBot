## 🎁 Rewards Formula

```
Total Stats Gained = Main Stats + Bonus Stats
Bonus SP = Total Stats / 10
```

Example:
- 800 Speed + 200 other stats = 1000 total
- Bonus: 1000 / 10 = 100 SP
- Uma gains: +800 Speed permanent!

## 🔢 Math Cheat Sheet

### Mood Improvement Chances:
- Rest: 40% chance +1 level
- Training success: 15% chance +1 level
- Training fail: 25% chance -1 level
- Weekly random: 10% chance ±1 level

### Optimal Strategy:
1. Maintain Good+ mood = 100% success
2. Train 6 days, rest 1 day
3. 60 total training days possible
4. Target: 1000+ total stats

### Skill ROI:
- Normal (100 SP): Break-even at ~20 trainings
- Rare (500 SP): Break-even at ~30 trainings
- Evolved (1000 SP): Break-even at ~40 trainings
- **Buy early for max value!**
